Rules of Celestedle

Guess the secret element of the day by entering its name. You'll receive feedback based on its attributes.

Categories

  • Collectibles: Items collected in-game. (strawberries, hearts....)
  • Characters: Characters of the story like Theo, Granny, Oshiro...
  • Environments: Entities without a major impact on the gameplay (Binoculars).
  • Mechanics: Entites that create the main gameplay gameplay (Crumble blocks).
  • Movement & Propulsion: Mechanics that you use to move around or gain speeds (move block, zippers...).
  • Hazards: Kills the player (Spikes, Spinners).

Colors Indicator

  • 🟩 Correct: Perfect match.
  • 🟨 Partial: Guess is part of the answer.
  • 🟧 Not Totally Wrong: Contains part the answer + extra.
  • 🟥 Wrong: Incorrect attribute.

Chapters

For the chapter indicator, only the A-sides are considered. For example, if an item is present in 3A, 3B, 3C and 8B, it will be displayed only as resort.
